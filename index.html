I am a graduate student at the Biomechanics and Control Lab at Yale University. I work on the biomechanics of the human foot, specifically, the contribution of the arches of the foot to its stiffness. I have a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from IIT Roorkee (B.Tech. 2014). In the past, I have worked at the Collective Interactions Unit at OIST, Japan (2015), the Learning Algorithms and Systems Lab at EPFL (2014), Switzerland, the Technical University of Hamburg-Harburg, Germany (2013) and NCBS-TIFR, India (2012).
